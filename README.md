# GTM Fit Scorer

> Score your startup's GTM readiness across 6 dimensions — with a concrete action plan.

**[→ Live Demo](https://iamtheaak.github.io/gtm-fit-scorer)**

---

Most early-stage startups fail not because the product is bad — but because the GTM is misaligned. Wrong channel for the stage. Untested messaging. ICP that's too broad to convert. This tool scores your GTM strategy before you scale it.

## What it scores

| Dimension | What it measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **ICP Clarity** | How precisely your ideal customer is defined |
| **Channel-Stage Fit** | Whether your primary channel matches your growth stage |
| **Message Clarity** | Whether your positioning has been tested with real buyers |
| **Differentiation** | How sharply you're differentiated from alternatives |
| **Model-Stage Fit** | Whether your business model creates friction at this stage |
| **Distribution Leverage** | Whether your product has built-in growth loops |

## Output

- Overall GTM readiness score (0–100)
- Per-dimension scores with specific diagnostic insights
- Visual radar chart of your readiness profile
- Prioritised action plan (high / medium / low)
- Channel-fit matrix comparing 5 GTM channels for your stage

## How to use

Fill in 8 fields about your startup → get results in under a second. No signup, no data sent anywhere.

## Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, JavaScript. Canvas API for the radar chart. No dependencies, no build step.

## Why I built this

After running GTM campaigns at Cosmofeed/SuperProfile and studying dozens of early-stage startup failures, I kept seeing the same pattern: founders optimise for tactics (which email template, which ad creative) before fixing strategy (wrong channel, unclear ICP, untested messaging). This tool forces the strategic questions first.
